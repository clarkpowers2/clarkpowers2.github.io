This Privacy Policy describes how HCCGSA LLC ("FreshSave Pro," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the FreshSave Pro web dashboard and mobile application (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

FreshSave Pro is operated by HCCGSA LLC, a limited liability company based in New York, New York, United States.

Contact: fdrns.clarke@cb2-verify.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password, stored in encrypted or hashed form. We never store your plain-text password.
  • Store name and display preferences

Store and Product Data

  • Product names, categories, prices, and expiry dates you enter
  • Discount and markdown history
  • Inventory and revenue-related data you choose to record in the Service

Payment Information

We use Stripe, Inc. to process subscription payments. Your full payment card details are collected and stored by Stripe, not by us. We receive limited billing information such as subscription status and the last four digits of your card.

Usage Information

We collect basic technical information necessary to operate the Service, such as login timestamps and actions taken within the app.

AI Assistant Interactions

When you use our in-app AI assistant ("Cosmo"), the questions you ask and the relevant store or product data needed to answer them are processed by our AI provider, OpenAI, to generate a response.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Process subscription payments and manage billing
  • Generate insights, analytics, and AI-assisted recommendations for your store
  • Communicate with you about your account, including verification and password-reset emails
  • Improve and troubleshoot the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share information only with third-party service providers who help us operate the Service, and only as necessary. These include:

  • Stripe, Inc. for payment processing
  • InsForge for backend database hosting and storage

Each of these providers processes data on our behalf under its own privacy and security commitments. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of FreshSave Pro, our users, or others.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted passwords, secure authentication, and access controls that restrict each account to only its own store's data.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes.
